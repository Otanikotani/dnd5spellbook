1st-level transmutation
Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you or a creature within 60 feet of you falls
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, M (a small feather or p iece o f down)
Duration: 1 minute

Choose up tofive falling creatures within range. A falling creature's rate of descent slows to 60 feet per round until the spell ends. If the creature lands before the spell ends, it takes nofalling damage and can land on its feet, and the spell ends for that creature.
